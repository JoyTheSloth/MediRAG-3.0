Slide 2: The Problem
-------------------
Title: The Medical AI Trust Crisis
- LLMs are overconfident: Hallucinations are safety hazards in healthcare.
- Blind trust in AI can lead to medical malpractice.
- Need for a "Forensic Audit Layer" between AI pipelines and clinicians.
- Solution: Automatic extraction, scoring, and verification of medical claims.

Slide 3: Project Overview
------------------------
Title: What is MediRAG-Eval 3.0?
- A high-fidelity Command Center for medical AI oversight.
- Key Functions:
  * MediChat AI: Standalone immersive chat interface.
  * Real-time Hallucination Risk Scoring (HRS).
  * Document-Aware Chat: Grounded answers from uploaded PDFs/Lab Reports.
  * AI Governance Dashboard: Compliance reporting and audit logs.

Slide 4: The 4-Layer Audit Engine
---------------------------------
Title: Forensic Verification Pipeline
1. Faithfulness Scorer: DeBERTa-v3 NLI checks for ground truth support.
2. Mistral 2-Pass Verification: Dual-call system for cross-examination.
3. Medical Entity Verifier: SciSpaCy + DrugBank (Dosage & Entity auditing).
4. Source Credibility Ranking: Evidence Tiering (Systematic Reviews to Case Reports).
5. Contradiction Detection: Internal NLI cross-checks for self-consistency.
