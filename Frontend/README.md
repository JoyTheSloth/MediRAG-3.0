## 🧐 "Wait, My AI is Lying to Me?"

Surprise! LLMs are basically that one friend who's really confident but actually knows nothing about medicine. In the medical world, a "creative" hallucination isn't just a bug; it's a lawsuit waiting to happen. 

**MediRAG-Eval** is the forensic audit layer that sits between your sophisticated RAG pipeline and the unsuspecting patient. We score faithfulness, entity accuracy, and source credibility so you don't have to explain to a board why your bot prescribed 5g of Lithium because the vector DB was having a bad day. 🙄

---

## ⚡ Key Features (The "Audit or Regret" List)

- **🎯 Target-Lock Auditing**: A high-tech "Medical Command Center" UI that actually makes you feel as cool as an infiltrator while you're just checking JSON blobs. 
- **🕵️ Forensic Scrambler**: Real-time visual feedback on "Hallucinations"—watch the text self-destruct and verify itself on the fly. 
- **🧠 4-Layer Audit Engine**: 
    1. **Faithfulness** (NLI-based grounding)
    2. **Entity Accuracy** (Because MET-formin isn't MET-hamphetamine)
    3. **Source Credibility** (Was the paper from The Lancet or a random Reddit thread?)
    4. **Internal Consistency** (Did it contradict itself in its own answer?)
- **📊 Real-time Dashboard**: Stunning glassmorphism gauges that turn amber when things get sketchy.

## 🎯 The Targeting System

We implemented a custom **GSAP-powered Target-Lock Cursor**. 
- **Idle**: It's a spinning crosshair.
- **Active**: It physically snaps its four corners to your buttons/links, letting you know it's ready to audit that specific data point.
- **Why?**: Because normal cursors are for boring apps. This is a *Command Center*. 🎖️
