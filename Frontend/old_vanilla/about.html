🎯 Our Mission

Building Trust in Medical AI.
One Audit at a Time.

Hallucinations in medical AI aren't just errors — they're patient safety risks. MediRAG-Eval was built to give developers, researchers, and AI governance teams a rigorous, open-source framework to detect, score, and explain hallucinations in RAG-based medical QA systems before they reach clinicians or patients.

THE PROBLEM
~30%

of LLM-generated medical answers contain at least one hallucinated claim — Ji et al., ACM Computing Surveys 2023

~12%

involve critical drug or dosage errors that could directly harm patients

Unverified Claims

LLMs confidently generate medical statements unsupported by retrieved evidence. Standard RAG pipelines have no mechanism to catch this.

Drug & Dosage Errors

Entity-level hallucinations — wrong drug names, incorrect dosages, contraindicated combinations — go undetected without domain-specific verification.

No Audit Trail

Existing RAG systems produce answers with no explainability layer. Regulators, clinicians, and developers have no way to trace why an answer was generated or how trustworthy it is.
