MediRAG-Eval 3.0: Key Feature Explanations (Simple English)
=========================================================

1. AI RESEARCH (The "Smart Library")
------------------------------------
Think of this as an expert researcher who has read over 100,000 medical 
books and papers. When you ask a question, the AI scans this huge 
"Smart Library" (PubMed and MedQA) to find the exact page or paragraph 
that answers your query. This ensures the answer is based on real 
science, not just a guess.

2. AI GOVERNANCE (The "Digital Auditor")
----------------------------------------
AI Governance is like having a strict supervisor watching the AI. 
It checks every answer to make sure it follows safety rules. If the 
AI tries to "hallucinate" (make stuff up), the Auditor catches it, 
blocks the answer, or forces it to try again using only verified 
facts. It keeps the AI honest and safe for doctors.

3. DASHBOARD (The "Control Tower")
----------------------------------
This is a visual screen where you can see all the "health stats" of the 
AI. It shows how many questions were asked, how many times the AI 
was almost wrong, and how high the "Health Risk Scores" are. It’s like 
a cockpit for hospital managers to monitor AI safety.

4. UPLOAD FEATURE (The "Personal Knowledge")
--------------------------------------------
You can drag and drop your own medical reports, lab results, or PDFs 
into the app. The AI "reads" these documents instantly and lets you 
ask questions about your specific data. It’s like giving the AI 
temporary access to your personal file so it can help you understand 
your reports better.

5. CHATS (The "Safe Conversation")
----------------------------------
The Chat section is where you talk to the AI. It looks like a normal 
chat app, but it has "superpowers":
- It shows you a "Safety Score" (HRS) for every answer.
- It gives you "Citation Cards" (links to the actual books it read).
- It warns you if it’s unsure about something.

6. API SECTION (The "Connector")
--------------------------------
The API is a way for other apps—like hospital software or pharmacy 
apps (e.g., Apollo or Tata 1mg)—to use our "Truth-Checking" system. 
It allows different medical platforms to connect to MediRAG and 
get safe, audited AI answers inside their own systems.
